Exploring Art Reflection

I learned a lot from this course about art and art history. I now have a greater appreciation for different types of art, some of the art I now like I used to not like or understand. By taking this course I now understand the meaning behind art and what art is. I value art more and see the art and beauty in all things. I liked learning about the history behind art and the different trends and art movements that took place. I love architecture and sculptures and want to travel to see monuments and statues. I really like modern art especially futurism and expressionism I like Earnest Ludwig Kirchner art and Umberto Boccioni work.
