Module 2 Discussion: Fav Media

My favorite medium to work with is watercolors, my grandmother is an amazing artist, and she has been teaching me how to watercolor. I love watercolors because I love how the colors blend and all the different variations of colors you can make and how pronounced or subtle you can show those colors on the paper. I wish I could share some of her work and what she has been teaching me, but I am currently moving and all that is packed up. Art pieces that inspire me is anything pottery or I love photography I did both those things in high school. I love art, but I have never found myself talent or creative maybe I just need more practice and need to be more disciplined to do that, I think everyone could be creative some may just need more work at it than others. I really appreciate others work and find it inspiring. I always say when I see something beautiful especially in nature, I wish I could capture what my eyes see because I can never get a picture to do it justice so I really appreciate those who can. My boyfriend and I are moving into our new home the end of July and I am already planning on decorating with many beautiful pots and beautiful works of art.
