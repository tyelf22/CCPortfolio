Reflection and Signature Assignment

  • Reflect on the evolution of your learning skills and strategies
    • How have you grown as a student? This semester I have grown because I have learned to be better with my time management, being better with my time has allowed me to receive better scores and my stress with school has been minimized.
    • What brought about those improvements? At the beginning of the semester, I set goals and expectations for myself to achieve and that is what really motivated me and helped me to improve.
    • What new study skills and strategies have you embraced that you will take with you to other learning environments? Every day I set aside time for each class I had, and I created a weekly schedule/ plan for myself so I could stay on track. Having a to do list really helped me stay focused. I will continue to plan and create schedules to follow to help with other classes. Another thing that has really helped me with my studies was I have become a better researcher and I am now able to read texts in a more beneficial way.
    • Connect these skills and strategies with specific activities, assignments, or readings. Critical chemistry was a helpful resource for me this semester really helped me thoroughly read the texts provided. I also really enjoyed the quizzes we took and how they were provided with a reading to go over before the quiz and that we could refer to it while taking the quiz.

  • Reflect on your new perspective regarding the world around you
    • How has your view of the world around you changed or been enhanced? I now understand the chemical reason behind phenomenon and chemistry is more a part of my daily life than I ever realized before this class. I will now better understand how the world around me works because of chemistry.
    • What new connections have you made because of your experience in this class? I know understand chemical reactions better and that chemistry is related to all things, math, cooking, cleaning, weather, our bodies, and nutrition.
    • In what ways, large and small, do you see the world differently now? I think chemistry has had a large impact on how I see the world differently, I now understand the how and why behind things, now when I see things, I will be able to look at it with a chemistry background/understanding.
    • Cite specific experiences in this course that prompted these changes. I really enjoyed the signature assignment and abstract assignment, it allowed me to research on topics that interested me and look at the chemical reason behind topics I was fascinated by. That helped me want to learn more about chemistry because I was able to apply it to my likes and personalize my learning about the topic.
