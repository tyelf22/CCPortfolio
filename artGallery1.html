Artist: Jena Schmidt
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 48’wx48’h

I love the gray and neutral muted colors. This is for sure something I would want to hang in my house. I love the lines and mountains it creates and the circle peace sign looking line that slashes through it is fine and subtle. So many details and colors you can get lost staring into it.
