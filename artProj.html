Module 3: Your creative art project

I chose to do a watercolor. I love all the different things you can do with the colors when you, watercolor. I chose monochromatic colors to show how much you can do with just one color. I painted the moon and water because I have always found the moon to be beautiful. I also love natural bodies of water. Watercolors are hard to master it has a mind of its own the colors flow where they want. I hope you enjoy my painting.
