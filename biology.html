Biology 1010

Salt Lake Community College | Summer 2021

Preflection

I am excited to learn more about biology and how it explains the natural world. I have always been fascinated by the natural world around me so I think this class would be a great opportunity to expand my knowledge and understanding of it. After I read the course syllabus it sparked my interest even more and I’m glad I decided to take this course. I am most excited to learn more about the evolution concept, patterns of inheritance and genetic information. I am also an advocate for the environment, so I am motivated to learn how to become more eco-friendly and how I can do my part to make the environment a better place. I can’t wait to learn about the different technological advances and how they have helped contribute to biology and its growth. I am eager to see how this class will benefit my future and how it provides more understanding and learning.

The challenges I anticipate with this course is the significant amount of information I need to retain and not being able to remember all I need to for exams and full understanding of the concepts. I plan to manage these concerns with a positive attitude, good study habits and resilience. Maintaining a good attitude is important and it is something I have learned with past courses, if you believe in yourself, you can accomplish anything you want to. Having a bad attitude will only discourage me and set me back. I will study daily and stay on top on coursework to avoid an overwhelming workload. I will take it a step at a time and in small bites to achieve my big goals. I will be resilient when disappointment may come my way and continue to try my best and work hard no matter what. With this plan I am confident I will have success in this course and achieve my goals.
