Module 5: Lovin' it

My two favorite periods is expressionism and futurism. With futurism I like that there is lots of interesting detail and shapes at make up the art. It kind of blows my mind and its different than anything else, it easy to know when looking at it the type of art it is. I like expressionism art because of the colors used and the stories the art can tell, I also just find it super beautiful to look at. 

My favorites from expressionism


Emil Nolde, Stormy Sea, 1930, Sprengel Museum in Hanover, Germany.

“Last Light” (2018), Alexandre Renoir 

Edvard Munch (1863-1944), “Bathing Man,” 

My favorites from futurism


Benedetta Cappa, Cime arse di solitudine, 1936 Museo dell’Aeronautica “Gianni Caproni”, Trento

Dynamic Hieroglyphic of the Bal Tabarin by Gino Severini

Sea = Dancer (Mare = Ballerin) by Gino Severini


Comments:

Heley I really enjoy the Keith Haring, Adhesive Wall Tiles, I like all the different shapes and how simple it is with the black and white yet complex from the fact you can get lost in the shapes. I feel like that has a lot of movement. I also liked Jackson Pollock, One (Number 31) I like how huge it is and I like the colors that were used. 

Madeline I really like Abstraktes Bild [599] - Gerhard Richter c.1986, I love the vibrant bold colors and the different shapes used, you could turn that painting to anything you wanted to with your mind. I also really like Mountains and Sea - Helen Frankenthaler c.1952. I like the light pastel colors and all the movement it has throughout the painting.
