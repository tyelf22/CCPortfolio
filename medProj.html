Medieval Manuscript Project

I chose a C for Chloe Coon I wanted to use a butterfly in my design because they’re colorful and fun. I did a dark black boarder around the edge to make it pop. I wanted my project full of color, so I surrounded my letter in purple. With so much color I wanted to separate everything with a white outline to emphasize everything more.

The Dimensions are 8”x11” and the format I used was a square. I wanted my butterfly to be full of color, so I used all color. I chose a gold for the C because it gave it a medieval look then I used yellow for the main area of the butterfly because that is my favorite color. Purple surrounded the whole letter because purple is a royal color which also gives a medieval feel. The materials I used was a permanent marker, gel pens and color pencils.
